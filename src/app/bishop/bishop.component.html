Bishop Cottons Boys' School

About Cottons

After standing tall for 150 glorious years, Bishop Cotton Boys’ School has won the accolade of the “Eton of the East”, and stands tall as Bangalore’s leading school, one of India’s three top-ranked schools, and the No: 1 among all metro schools in India, with students of the school who have gone on to shine in different walks of life, all over the world.

Life at Cottons

Cottons has always stood for discipline and the molding of character of its students to ensure that it will not crumble when tested in the crucible of adulthood. This institution prides itself in imparting quality education while preparing them for the competitive world outside the classroom via its meticulously envisioned house system.
